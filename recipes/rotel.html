Rotel

Description

A sausage dip that's easy and cheesy? This 3-ingredient sausage dip comes together in minutes and is sure to be a hit at your next party or get-together. Smooth, melty Velveeta, zesty Ro-Tel diced tomatoes and green chiles, and savory ground beef team up to impress in this delicious dip. Serve with tortilla chips or your favorite side.

Ingredients

  • Velveeta cheese
  • Rotel cans
  • Ground beef or meat of choice

Steps

  1. Cook meat on stove
  2. Drain grease from the meat
  3. Open rotel cans and open cheese
  4. turn the heat down to medium
  5. Put rotel and cheese into pot
  6. Stir until melted
